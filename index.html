Recognition of Indian license plate number from live stream videos

Machine Learning approach to recognize license plate from live steram videos. We have selected OpenALPR, k-NN and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) based techniques, adapted them for local conditions, compared their accuracy on both still images and live stream videos. Our analysis demonstrates that the CNN based approach is the most suitable for recognizing number plates in India.
